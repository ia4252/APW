Swanky Studios - 3D Modeling and Animation Division

Welcome to the Swanky Studios 3D Modeling and Animation department. Here, we take ideas sketched in notebooks and on napkins and turn them into tangible, detailed, and high-fidelity 3D models. Then, we bring them to life.       

Learn more »

Current Projects

3D Modeling and Animating the entirety of a brand new game (from scratch) is a huge task. There are always more assets to bring to life.

  • Arachnid - "The Mannequin"   
    "The Mannequin" is one of the main horror-based characters of "Arachnid." Taking on the shape of an old, worn down mannequin, "The Mannequin" stalks its victoms, moving only when it is not being looked at.    
  • Arachnid -  "The Backpack"

    "The Backpack" is one of the key components in the gameplay in "Arachnid." Serving as the player's inventory, the player will access a physical backpack whenever checking his/her inventory.     

  • Arachnid - "The Arachnid"
    "The Arachnid" serves as the main horror-based character in "Arachnid." Whilst stalking its victoms, "The Arachnid" can be sighted in a form resembling that of a fleshy, human-spider amalgamation.
